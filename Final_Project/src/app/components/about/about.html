What is VALORANT?

VALORANT is a free-to-play tactical hero shooter developed and published by Riot Games. It combines precise gunplay with unique agent abilities in a competitive 5v5 environment.

Development Team

VALORANT is developed by Riot Games, the creators of League of Legends. The development team includes veterans from various top-tier game studios, bringing together expertise in competitive gaming and tactical shooters.
